Slide titled "Prevalence of Coronary Artery Disease Increases with PRS" (slide 31).

Panel C: Scatter plot showing:
  - X-axis: Percentile of polygenic score (0 to 100)
  - Y-axis: Prevalence of CAD (%) ranging from 0 to ~11%

Data points (dark blue dots) show a monotonically increasing and accelerating trend. Prevalence is ~1% at low PRS percentiles and rises sharply to ~11% at the 100th percentile, illustrating a strong association between high polygenic risk score and coronary artery disease prevalence.

Source: Khera et al (2018) Nature Genetics.
